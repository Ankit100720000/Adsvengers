CASE STUDIES

01
Luxury Real Estate · NCR
₹20 Cr+ in Real Estate Business — Premium Projects, NCR

We managed end-to-end performance campaigns for multiple premium residential projects across Noida and Greater Noida, targeting ticket sizes of ₹3.5 Cr+. The challenge: generate high-intent site-visit leads in a saturated luxury market dominated by large developer budgets.

Our team built precision targeting stacks using lookalike audiences, behavioural interest layers, and video-led creatives. We implemented a full funnel from awareness to WhatsApp conversion, with weekly creative rotations to prevent ad fatigue.

02
EdTech · Performance Marketing
18.69x ROAS in a Single Month — Online Course Platform

An EdTech platform offering courses in Data Analytics, Business Analytics, Product Management, and Generative AI needed to maximise enrollments within a fixed monthly budget. Previous campaigns were generating leads at high CPL with poor conversion to paid enrollments.

We rebuilt the campaign architecture from the ground up — aggressive creative testing, tightened audience segmentation, and landing pages rebuilt around one core conversion action. The result: CPL reduced by 60% while scaling spend.
